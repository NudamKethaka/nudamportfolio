The Challenge

Earthly Goods approached me with a need to completely revamp their visual identity. As a company focused on sustainable, minimalist products, their existing branding didn't reflect their core values. The challenge was to create a brand system that communicated environmental consciousness while maintaining a premium, minimalist aesthetic.

The Solution

I developed a comprehensive brand identity centered around organic shapes, earthy tones, and clean typography. The logo mark combines abstract leaf motifs with geometric precision, representing the balance between nature and human design. The color palette uses muted greens and browns derived from natural pigments, with crisp white space to create breathing room.

Typography System

The typography combines a clean sans-serif (Inter) for readability with a subtle serif (Lora) for premium touchpoints. This duality reflects the brand's balance between accessibility and sophistication.

Color Palette

The primary palette consists of three earthy tones complemented by a warm white and deep charcoal. These colors were specifically chosen for their natural origins and print reproducibility.

Visual Language

The visual language extends beyond just logo and colors. I developed a system of organic shapes that can be used as patterns, frames, or decorative elements. These shapes are derived from the logo mark but can be adapted flexibly across various applications.

Packaging System

A key component was developing sustainable packaging that aligned with the brand values. The solution uses recycled materials with minimal ink coverage, focusing on embossing and debossing for tactile quality. The packaging system is modular, allowing various product sizes while maintaining visual consistency.

"Nudam completely transformed our brand identity. The designs were not only beautiful but perfectly captured our company's values and mission. The packaging system alone reduced our material costs by 15% while improving customer perception."

Results

Since launching the new brand identity, Earthly Goods has seen a 40% increase in brand recognition and a 25% uplift in sales. The packaging system won a Sustainable Design Award and has been featured in several design publications.
