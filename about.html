About Me

Get to know the person behind the designs – my journey, approach, and the principles that guide my creative process.

My Story

Hi, I'm Nudam Kethaka, a graphic designer with a passion for creating meaningful visual experiences that connect brands with their audiences. My journey in design began over a decade ago when I discovered the power of visual communication during my time at the School of Visual Arts in New York.

After graduating, I honed my skills working with various design studios and agencies before establishing my independent practice five years ago. This journey has shaped my approach to design – one that values simplicity, purpose, and the power of visual storytelling.

Today, I collaborate with forward-thinking clients across industries, helping them articulate their vision through thoughtful, strategic design solutions. Whether it's crafting a brand identity, designing a user interface, or creating print materials, my goal remains the same: to create designs that not only look beautiful but also solve real problems.

Design Philosophy

Purpose-Driven

Great design solves problems. I believe every design decision should serve a purpose and contribute to achieving your business goals.

Balance & Harmony

I strive for designs that feel balanced and harmonious, creating visual experiences that feel natural and intuitive to the viewer.

Thoughtful Simplicity

I believe in distilling complex ideas to their essence. Simple doesn't mean boring – it means clear, focused, and impactful.
