Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process like?

My design process typically involves discovery (understanding your goals and audience), research, conceptualization, design development, revisions based on feedback, and final delivery. I believe in collaboration throughout the process to ensure the final result aligns perfectly with your vision.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A brand identity project might take 4-6 weeks, while a smaller project like a poster design could be completed in 1-2 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific needs.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need to understand your business/project goals, target audience, design preferences, competitor insights, and any existing brand materials. I provide a detailed brief questionnaire to all new clients to ensure I have all the necessary information.

Do you offer ongoing design support?

Yes! After completing your project, I'm available for ongoing design support and maintenance. Many clients choose to work with me on a retainer basis for consistent brand management and regular design needs.
